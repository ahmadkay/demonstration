About Us

Our History

Abi private school commence operations on 29 november, 1999 in Badariya at its temporary site with seven children and two teachers. based on excellent academic perpormane of the school we experience rapid increase in the enrolment of new children coupled with determination and courage of our purpose, we ware able to move to our permanent site on 7 may 2000.

Abi private school has sixteen class rooms in primary section and twelve in secondary section. manage by thirty five teachers and six supporting staff. the enrolment of our pupils as at today morethan 400 in the school. the aim of establishing the school was to promote the development of education generally in the state and also to contribute to the success of the federal government policies on universal basic education.

Incompormity to meeting academic standard we recieve inspectors from various places like LGEA zonal education office, primary school management board, federal ministry of education and other non governmental oganizations. having satisfied with the entire requirment for the awaard for the award of certificate to operate as stipulated by the relevant authorities and the excelent facilities on the ground, the school got it provisional approval on the 23 August, 2000, final approval on the 2 may, 2002. our secodary sections opens on 25 july, 2006 with well equiped laboratory and computer room, with the help of some parents we are able to add more computer system

The school is doing well in supporting and other educational competiton where we always come out succesful. the school got meritorious awards from nierian turkish international collage for taking the second position in the 2007 kebbi state mathemathics competition. also on the year 2005, the school took first position in the national common entrance examination (NCEE) in the state.

The school started graduating students in 2001. our first set were 100% in the national common entrane examinations (NCEE) and got 100% in the interview of both federal and state common entrance examinations. while in 2002 90%, 2003 90%, 2004 100%, 2005 98%. our belief is that the school will continue to attain greather heights

Also in the year 2012, the school took second position in the west african examination council (WAEC). in the year 2014 also the school took first position in WAEC.

Abi started NECO in the year 2012. in the year 2013, our student Maliki Damuna won the best student in NAFDAC competition in junior category, and in the year 2014, SS students won second position in NAFDAC competition. in 2016, our SS students won the third position in NYSC mathemathics competiton. on 24/02/2018 our SS students went for sports competition organize in solid science Birnin Kebbi and won the second position, and also on 28/02/2018, our SS students went for mathematics foundation movement competition an won second position.
